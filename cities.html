Olympia

Olympia is the capital of the U.S. state of Washington and the county seat of Thurston County.

Seattle

Seattle, a city on Puget Sound in the Pacific Northwest, is surrounded by water, mountains, and evergreen forests.

Vancouver

Vancouver is a city on the north bank of the Columbia River in the U.S. state of Washington.

Quick Facts

  1. Olympia was incorporated on January 28, 1859, and named after the Olympic Mountains, which are visible to the northwest.

  2. Seattle is home to the first Starbucks, which opened in 1971 in Pike Place Market.

  3. Vancouver is the fourth-largest city in Washington state.
