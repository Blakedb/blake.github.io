About Olympia

Olympia is the capital of the U.S. state of Washington and the county seat of Thurston County. It is known for its natural beauty and historical significance.

Quick Facts

  • Population: Approximately 55,000
  • Founded: 1859
  • Named After: Olympia, the mythical home of the Greek gods
  • State Capital Since: 1853
  • Population Classification: Urban
  • Avg Income of Washington Resident: $90,325
  • Avg Income of Olympia Resident: $85,315
