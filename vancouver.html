About Vancouver

Vancouver is a city on the north bank of the Columbia River in the U.S. state of Washington. It is part of the Portland-Vancouver metropolitan area and has a rich history and a growing economy.

Quick Facts

  • Population: Approximately 185,000
  • Founded: 1825
  • Named After: George Vancouver, a British explorer
  • Population Classification: Urban
  • Avg Income of Washington Resident: $90,325
  • Avg Income of Vancouver Resident: $75,435
